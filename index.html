The First Message

It all began on July 10, at 8:00 PM, with a simple sticker sent through Discord. Just a friendly "hi" that would change everything.

We immediately connected over our last Valorant match, where she played as Sage and I was Reyna.

Then came the moment of truth. I called her "babe" playfully, and she quickly said she couldn't be called that because she's gay. My response? "Who said I'm not gay too?"

"And just like that, a connection was formed from a simple hello."

Discovering Aki

After a week or two, she called me "baby" for the first time. It caught me completely off guard—I didn't know how to respond at first. But soon, it became the most comforting exchange between us.

Our little conversations began every single day. Through these chats, I discovered:

  • Her nickname is Aki (she's cautious about sharing personal details)
  • Her birthday is May 16, and she's 25 years old
  • She loves cats and dogs equally
  • She dreams of traveling to Europe, Paris, Italy, Switzerland and beyond

She shared that this was her first time taking a risk letting someone of the same gender court her. That vulnerability made everything feel even more special.
