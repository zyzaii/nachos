à mon très cher

To you,

Red surrounds us, hearts everywhere —
a sign that Valentine's is in the air.
So I write this letter, hoping you'll see,
a formal request, from the heart of me.
I ask your permission, kind and true,
to be my Valentine date — just us two.
will you grace this valentine’s eve?
Would you be my Valentine?
ever faithfully
A thousand thanks

yes

you have made this Valentine’s Day unforgettable.

— with deepest admiration —

break the seal with a click
